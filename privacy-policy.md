## Introduction

Welcome to Recollect (recollect.so), a comprehensive bookmark, image, and document management platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Password**: Encrypted and securely stored for account access
- **Display Name**: Optional display name for your profile
- **Username**: Unique username for your account
- **Profile Information**: Any additional profile details you choose to provide

### Content You Create

- **Bookmarks**: URLs, titles, descriptions, and metadata of saved bookmarks
- **Files**: Images, videos, documents, and other files you upload
- **Categories**: Custom categories and collections you create
- **Tags**: Custom tags you create and apply to content
- **Notes and Descriptions**: Any notes or descriptions you add to your content

### Usage Information

- **Activity Data**: How you interact with the platform (searches, views, edits)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes
- **Session Data**: Login sessions and authentication tokens

### AI-Generated Content

- **Image Captions**: AI-generated descriptions of your images
- **OCR Text**: Text extracted from images using AI
- **Content Analysis**: AI analysis of your bookmarks and files
- **Search Embeddings**: Vector representations of your content for AI search

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Service Functionality

- **Content Management**: Store, organize, and manage your bookmarks and files
- **Search and Discovery**: Provide search functionality and content recommendations
- **Collaboration**: Enable sharing and collaboration features
- **Synchronization**: Keep your data synchronized across devices

### AI and Automation Features

- **Content Analysis**: Use AI to analyze and categorize your content
- **Image Processing**: Generate captions and extract text from images
- **Semantic Search**: Provide AI-powered search capabilities
- **Smart Organization**: Suggest categories and tags for your content

### Service Improvement

- **Analytics**: Understand how the service is used to improve functionality
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor service performance and reliability
- **Error Tracking**: Identify and fix technical issues
- **Feature Development**: Develop new features based on usage patterns

### Communication

- **Service Updates**: Send important service notifications
- **Collaboration Invites**: Send invitations for shared categories
- **Account Security**: Notify you of security-related events
